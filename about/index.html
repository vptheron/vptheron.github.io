About Me

I'm Vincent Theron. I was born in Paris, France and now work as a software engineer in Boston, MA.

I earned my Master's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Université Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée in June 2008. I started developing software professionally in 2007, and since then, I have had the opportunity to work in a lot of different industries and to build all kinds of applications (from online sport betting platform to real time trading software to social search engines).
