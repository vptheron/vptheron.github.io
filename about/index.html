About Me

My name is Vincent Theron. I am a software engineer and author based in Boston, MA.

Born and raised in Paris, France, I earned my Master's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Université Paris-Est Marne-la-Vallée in June 2008. I started developing software professionally in 2007. I have had the chance to work in a lot of different industries (such as ad-tech, finance, online gambling) in many different countries (United States, France, Sweden).

I have worked with several programming languages such as Scala, Java, Ruby, Python. Other technologies I am exploring on my spare time include Clojure, Elixir and Haskell.

I am the co-author of Scala High Performance Programming published by Pack Publishing.

When I am not in front of the keyboard, my other interests include soccer, ice hockey and outdoor activities such as hiking and snowshoeing.
